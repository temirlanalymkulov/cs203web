The Idea of

AutoBot is

a new mobile application based on ANDROID and IOS to search, order, purchase, sell and deliver auto parts to all car brands available on the local market using the VIN code (Vehicle Identification Number) of cars, as well as the identification number of spare parts, or by keywords and names of car parts. Currently, the entire process of searching, buying and selling, delivering auto parts takes place directly in the market or auto parts stores, which in turn creates great inconvenience, taking a lot of time, money and energy from the buyer (car owner), and creating the need for the seller to keep the shops or an entire auto parts store. Another important factor in creating a mobile application with a database of auto parts is its availability anywhere in the world since buyers (car owners) from remote regions of our country are not able to order spare parts directly from Bishkek, sometimes they have to come for spare parts themselves. At the same time, similar programs, websites and mobile applications have already been created and are successfully operating in many developed countries. This mobile application is convenient and beneficial for both the buyer and the seller and is designed to simplify peopleâ€™s lives.
